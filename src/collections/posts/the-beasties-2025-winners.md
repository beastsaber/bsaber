# The BeastSaber Mapping Awards Winners: 2025

2,000+ total submissions, 300+ mappers and lighters. 194 finalists. 95 winners!
\
Shout out to those that made it to the final [nominees](/posts/the-beasties-2025-nominees)! And congratulations to all of the winners in this year’s BeastSaber Mapping Awards! Trophies, medals, and ribbons will be on their way to winners soon!

> The BeastSaber Mapping Awards relies on community donations to provide real, physical awards for winners! If you want to help provide a steady form of support for The Beasties, please consider visiting our [**Patreon**](https://www.patreon.com/BeastSaberNews) and contributing to any tier! All contributions are appreciated! ❤️
